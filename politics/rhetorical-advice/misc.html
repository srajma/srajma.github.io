8. Eggspoze aesthetic

Also published on Twitter

RWs need to better-tailor their use of EGGSPOZE aesthetic.

EGGSPOZE aesthetic is for *news*—to show that you, a formerly neutral party just like the reader, are learning shocking revelations for the first time.

For everything else, just state it as established knowledge: signals that you know what you’re talking about. Using EGGSPOZE aesthetic for what you supposedly always knew signals under-confidence.
